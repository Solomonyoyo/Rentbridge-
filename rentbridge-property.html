About this property

A refined space in the heart of VI

This beautifully finished 2-bedroom apartment is located in one of Victoria Island's most sought-after streets. The unit has been fully renovated with quality fittings, including a modern kitchen, tiled floors throughout, and built-in wardrobes in both bedrooms. The estate offers 24/7 power supply via a dedicated generator backup, constant water supply from an estate borehole, and round-the-clock security with CCTV coverage. The apartment is ideal for working professionals, couples, or small families seeking a quiet but centrally located Lagos address. It is a 5-minute drive to Onikan, 10 minutes to Lekki Phase 1, and walking distance to major banks, restaurants, and supermarkets. The landlord is highly responsive and open to negotiation on agency and legal fees for serious tenants.
